Please review below before installing

CryoTracker is not digitally signed. CryoTracker is intended to be a free application and digital certificates are too expensive for a free application. This means that Windows and web browsers will warn you that it may be harmful.

Below are some of the warnings you may be presented with while downloading and installing CryoTracker on your computer.

  • Web browsers may warn you that you may have downloaded a harmful file.
  • Your virus protection software could potentially flag it as a threat.
  • Windows SmartScreen: This is a check to see if the application is signed with a digital certificate. If it does not have one it will let you know it is from an "Unknown Publisher". Click "More Info", then "Run Anyway" to proceed.
  • User Account Control: This is another check which basically gives you one last warning that it is from an "Unknown Publisher". Click "Yes" to proceed.
